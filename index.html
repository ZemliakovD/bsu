ABOUT US

The Department of Systems Analysis and Computer Simulation (former name is Department of Systems Analysis) was founded in 1988 as a structural unity of the Faculty of Radiophysics and Computer Technologies (former name is Radio Physics and Electronics), Belarusian State University.

Within the university education system the Department of Systems Analysis and Computer Simulation provides general faculty courses, including lectures and practical courses, for the B.Sc., M.Sc. and Ph.D. students of the Belarusian State University.

The department maintains cooperation with the National Academy of Sciences, the Republican Scientific and Practical Center of Oncology and Radiology, the Republican Scientific and Practical Center for Pediatric Oncology, Hematology and Immunology. Among core collaborators from foreign universities and scientific laboratories can be highlighted the Luxembourg Institute of Health (Luxembourg), the research and production company EuroPhoton GmbH (Germany), the universities of Wageningen and Groningen (Netherlands), the independent University of Amsterdam (Netherlands), the University of Luxembourg, the Max Planck Institute dynamics of complex technical systems (Marburg, Germany), Norwegian Institute of Natural Sciences (Os, Norway), University of Bochum (Germany). Internships for teachers and are organized regularly.
